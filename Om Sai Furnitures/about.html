About

Who We Are

"Om Sai Furnitures" is one of the fastest growing plywood furniture company, a premium brand of furnitures, an ISO:9001, FOS13423.

Foundation

Under the dynamic guidance of company's Chairman Mr. Shivdhari Vishwakarma, Om Sai Furnitures started its journey from 2005- with a vision to build an extraordinary organization that would create a long lasting value for it's customers, business associates and contribute towards the Make in India, Skilled India Mission.

Market Position

Today Om Sai Furnitures is emerging as one of the major players in the global market with it's presence in more than 11 countries and boast of 55 destributors, 2500 dealesrs and a sales team of 200+ Pan India.
